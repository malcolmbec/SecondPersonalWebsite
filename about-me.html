Hi, I'm Becca!

Game. Design. Development.

Ask me about my projects! My weekends are kept pretty busy with game jams, hackathons, and side quests. My favorite completed project is Sleepy Jeoff from Global Game Jam 2019! Our artist team was amazing and having only two programmers meant I learned a lot in a short time.

I grew up in suburban MA with family across New England. I was co-president of my high school's NERD club and I'm now Social Chair for the Northeastern Game Development Club. Recently, I've been playing a lot of story-driven action-adventure games. I am a 3rd-year Computer Science student, minoring in Game Design!
